About Us

The Barangay Tagapo Cleaning Team in the City of Santa Rosa, Laguna, since its establishment in 2023, the team has been committed to improving the local environment, specifically by helping to control flooding and reduce mosquito populations.

This initiative was launched by Barangay Captain Etaw Caravana and their ongoing services include Lamok Busters, Clean-Up Drives, and Adopt-A-Lot.

Our Impact

Through community efforts and dedication, we are shaping a greener, more sustainable future for all.

01 Urban Greening

Actively preserve green spaces, reduces the risk of falling trees during storms, and help build climate resilience through planting initiatives.

02 Biodiversity Care

By cultivating both plants and fish, the barangay is supporting biodiversity in a controlled environment.

03 Sustainable Living

Reduces disease, improve sanitation, and encourage community participation promoting healthier community.

04 Smart Waste Disposal

By sorting and salvaging reusable materials, the team minimizes landfill contributions, indirectly supporting energy efficiency and resource conservation.
